About

hi, I'm Lauren

mat pilates instructor, nutritionist, and the person who decided a greenhouse was the right place to teach a class.

how I got here

I've never really been able to get on board with fads. trends come and go, but the body doesn't change that fast, and most of what's marketed as a quick fix doesn't hold up once you look at it closely.

that's the lens that shaped how I came to pilates and nutrition. I'm a certified mat pilates instructor, and I hold a nutrition certification through the Nutrition Institute of Canada. I teach movement and think about food the same way: food-first, not fear-first, and nothing that asks you to cut out an entire category of life to feel good.

I've taught in community centres, and last fall and winter I taught in a greenhouse. now I'm out on the grounds at Harford's for the warmer months. there's something about practicing next to things that are actually growing that fits the way I want people to feel in class: unhurried, a little more connected to where they are.

Credentials
  • BSc, Biochemistry
  • Certified Mat Pilates Instructor
  • Nutrition Certification, Nutrition Institute of Canada
My approach

food-first, not fad-first. movement that meets you where you are. no products to sell you, just a class to show up to.
